# Week 7 — Challenges

The exercises drill basics. **Challenges stretch you.** Each one takes 60–120 minutes and produces something you can commit to your portfolio.

## Index

1. **[Challenge 1 — Build a spec-faithful screen](./challenge-01-spec-faithful-screen.md)** — rebuild a provided design spec pixel-faithfully as a typed, reusable component with real touch targets, accessibility, and loading/empty states. No `any`. (~90 min)

Challenges are optional. If you skip them, you can still pass the week. If you do them, you'll be measurably ahead — and the component-with-states discipline here is exactly what Week 8 (navigation) and Week 9 (real data) build on top of. The reusable card and the loading/empty/content pattern you produce reappear in the mini-project and again in the capstone.
